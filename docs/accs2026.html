Jamf: Set It and Forget It

Presented at ACCS 2026 at Christopher Newport University

This talk covered how to streamline Mac app deployment using Jamf Pro's App Catalog and App Installers. The goal: set up your apps once and let Jamf handle the packaging, versioning, and updates automatically. Less time wrangling installers, more time on the work that matters.
