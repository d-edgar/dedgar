Privacy Policy

Stoked — Streak & Countdown Tracker

The short version

Stoked does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data collection

Stoked does not collect any personal information. Your streaks, countdowns, milestones, and settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. No data is transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

Analytics and tracking

Stoked does not use any analytics frameworks, tracking pixels, or third-party SDKs. There is no usage tracking of any kind.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are scheduled locally on your device. No push notification servers or external services are involved.

Third-party services

Stoked does not integrate with any third-party services. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no account systems.

Children's privacy

Stoked does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is ever updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
