About

IT Leader & Mac Admin

David has been working in IT since 2006, when he began his degree at Old Dominion University. A computer hobbyist and technology enthusiast for as long as he can remember, he turned that passion into a career spanning nearly two decades across endpoint management, automation, and infrastructure.

For the past seven-plus years he's led and grown teams in his department, combining hands-on technical skills with people-focused leadership. He specializes in Apple device management with Jamf Pro and loves building tools that make life easier for IT teams.

Outside of work, David is an avid runner and enjoys sharing knowledge with the Mac admin community at conferences like ACCS.
