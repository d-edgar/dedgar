Common questions

Where is my data stored?

All your streaks, countdowns, and milestones are stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.

How do I change my notification time?

Open Stoked, go to Settings, and tap the reminder time to choose when you'd like to be notified.

Can I recover a deleted streak?

Unfortunately, once a streak is deleted it can't be recovered. We recommend being careful with the delete button!

Is Stoked free?

Yes. No subscriptions, no accounts, no ads.
